Welcome to EDC

Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) is a club that helps budding entrepreneurs come out with their ideas and innovations and to gain knowledge about the procedure and the struggles an entrepreneur goes through during a startup and after the company has been established.
EDC is committed to the cause of encouraging entrepreneurship among students. The cell invites various renowned entrepreneurs to share their experiences. Lectures and awareness programs are regularly conducted to enlighten the students about the joys and problems of entrepreneurship.
Guest lectures are conducted throughout the year to involve the students in the various activities.
